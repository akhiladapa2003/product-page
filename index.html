Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about TimeX SmartWatch.

How long does the battery last?

The battery life varies by model. The Basic version offers 5 days, the Premium version offers 7 days, and the Pro version offers up to 10 days on a single charge with normal usage. Battery life may vary based on usage patterns, features used, and other factors.

Is the smartwatch compatible with both iOS and Android?

Yes, TimeX SmartWatch is compatible with both iOS 12.0+ and Android 8.0+ devices. You'll need to download our companion app from the App Store or Google Play to access all features.

What health features does the watch offer?

TimeX SmartWatch offers comprehensive health monitoring including heart rate tracking, blood oxygen saturation (SpO2), sleep analysis, stress level monitoring, and more. The Premium and Pro versions also include advanced features like ECG monitoring and breathing exercises.

How waterproof is the watch?

TimeX SmartWatch has a 5 ATM water resistance rating, which means it can withstand pressures equivalent to about 50 meters underwater. This makes it suitable for swimming, showering, and water-based activities. However, we recommend checking the specific model's specifications as water resistance may vary.

Can I answer calls directly from the watch?

Yes, the Premium and Pro versions of TimeX SmartWatch support cellular connectivity, allowing you to make and receive calls directly from your watch when paired with your smartphone. The Basic version can only notify you of incoming calls.
